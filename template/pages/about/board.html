Board of directors

As of 2026-01-29, the board consists of the following directors:

Magnus Hagander (President)

Elected: 2024-02-27, end of term: 2027-02-26
Contact: mha(at)postgresql.eu

Magnus has been involved with PostgreSQL one way or another since last century, as a user, developer and is these days involved in many different aspects of managing the Open Source project as well. He's a founding director of PostgreSQL Europe, and is involved to different extents in many of the different events around Europe and elsewhere.

Andreas 'ads' Scherbaum (Vice President)

Elected: 2025-02-27, end of term: 2028-02-26
Contact: ascherbaum(at)postgresql.eu

Andreas lives with his family in Germany, works with PostgreSQL since 1997, is part of the organization team for a number of PostgreSQL conferences, and likes to work on very large data in PG. He also loves ice cream.

Ilya Kosmodemiansky (Treasurer)

Co-opted: 2025-05-09, end of term: See note below
Contact: ik(at)postgresql.eu

Ilya Kosmodemiansky is a CEO and founder at Data Egret. A consultancy specializing in PostgreSQL migration, maintenance and support. Ilya has a broad experience working with PostgreSQL as consultant, architect and administrator. His main focus is database performance and optimization. He sees the mission of PostgreSQL in substituting the commercial databases in high-performance mission-critical applications.

Stefan Fercot (Vice Treasurer)

Co-opted: 2026-01-29, end of term: See note below
Contact: sfercot(at)postgresql.eu

Stefan has more than 15 years of experience working with PostgreSQL in production environments. His work focuses on database operations, reliability, and architecture, helping organizations run PostgreSQL safely at scale. He has a particular interest in backup and recovery, regularly advises on backup strategy and high-availability setups, and has volunteered for several PostgreSQL Europe activities over the years.

Stefan works at Data Egret and lives in Belgium. He enjoys sharing practical PostgreSQL knowledge through community involvement, blog posts, and events.

Note: Stefan was co-opted to the board on 29 January 2026 by the remaining directors following the resignation of Karen Jex. His co-option will be put to the members for ratification at the next General Meeting, and if ratified, he will serve the remainder of Ms. Jex's original term.

Dave Page (Secretary)

Elected: 2023-02-28, end of term: 2026-02-27
Contact: dpage(at)postgresql.eu

Dave has been contributing to the PostgreSQL project since the 1990's, working on the database server, ODBC and .NET drivers, installers, website & project infrastructure, and more over the years. In 1997 he started the pgAdmin project and continues to lead it today. Dave serves on the PostgreSQL Core Team, as the Chairman of the PostgreSQL Community Association, and as Secretary of PostgreSQL Europe.

Dave works at pgEdge as Chief Technology Officer, and enjoys spending time with his family, playing bass guitar, and fishing when he's not working.
